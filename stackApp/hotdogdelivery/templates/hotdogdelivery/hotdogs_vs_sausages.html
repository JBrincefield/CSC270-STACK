About — Our Mission

At Hotdog Delivery our goal is simple: deliver honest, great-tasting sausages and hotdogs with transparent ingredients and playful packaging.

  • No additives or preservatives — just quality meat and simple spices.
  • Sustainable packaging experiments (we like the idea of playful, recyclable boxes).
  • Support local suppliers and keep the community fed.

Hotdogs vs Sausages — The Difference

Hotdogs are typically seasoned and blended for a smooth texture and consistent, grill-ready profile. Sausages often celebrate a coarser grind, bolder spices, and regional character. We respect both traditions and deliver on both fronts.

We also believe in transparency: if you want sausages with no additives or preservatives, we make that promise. Our packaging experiments are playful but we always prioritize food safety and sustainability.
